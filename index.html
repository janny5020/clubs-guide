Clubs at Duolingo

Your quick guide to joining, starting, and getting reimbursed for club activities.

Open to all Duos Any location $100 / month

What are clubs?

TL;DR — Clubs are interest-based communities open to all Duos. Attend activities, spend up to $100/month, and get reimbursed via Navan. No formal membership or commitment required.

Social communities

Interest-based groups organized by any Duo, in any location — running, wine tasting, crafting, and more.

No commitment needed

No formal membership. Show up to events you care about, skip the ones you don't.

Open to everyone

All clubs must be open and accessible to all Duos — no exclusive cliques.

Find them on Slack

Search for #club-* channels. Check #post-club-events-pgh or #post-club-events-nyc for upcoming events.

The budget

$100
per Duo · per month
The budget follows the Duo, not the club. Spread it across multiple clubs — e.g. $20 wine + $15 crafting + $10 running = $45 total.

EMEA locations: 100 EUR or 100 GBP based on local currency.

What's reimbursable?

Covered
  • Tickets & AdmissionConcerts, tours, marathons, park passes
  • Food & DrinksMeals and drinks at club gatherings
  • ClassesFitness, crafting, cooking (not in Learning Budget)
  • Equipment RentalsKayaks, skis, climbing shoes for events
  • Travel to EventsGas, Lyft, train, flights
  • Guests (up to 50%)Half your monthly spend can cover a guest
Not Covered
  • Personal EquipmentRunning shoes, individual gear
  • Swag / ClothingIndividual branded items
  • Medical CareGroup therapy or medical events
  • Cause-Driven EventsPolitical or opinion-based activities
  • Late SubmissionsExpenses submitted past Finance deadlines

How to start a club

  1. 1

    Complete the Club Registration Form

    Fill out the Club Registration Google Form. Track your approval status in columns J & K of this spreadsheet.

  2. 2

    Designate a Club Leader

    Pick a leader (or two). They don't need to run every event — just be the general point of contact for the club.

  3. 3

    Create a Public Slack Channel

    Must use the club- prefix (e.g. #club-yoga). Need a rename? Submit a ticket via #help-it.

  4. 4

    Reach 10 Members (5 outside PGH)

    Recruit via #random, #post-club-events-pgh, or send a rep to the next Club Fair.

Quick FAQs

Is the $100 per Duo or per club?▼
Per Duo. You can spread it across multiple clubs in the same month — e.g. $20 wine + $15 crafting = $35 total.
Can I bring a guest?▼
Yes. Up to 50% of your monthly spend can go toward a guest. Guests must participate with you and don't count toward headcount minimums. Multiple Duos can't apply their budget toward the same guest.
Do language tables count as clubs?▼
No — language tables are separately funded and operate independently from the clubs program.
Can I buy club swag?▼
No individual swag is covered. Shared reusable club gear stored at the office may qualify with prior Workplace approval.
What about Health & Wellness clubs?▼
Follow the additional discretion guidelines. Duolingo cannot reimburse events deemed medical care (e.g. group therapy). See full guidance →
How do I rename my Slack channel to have the club- prefix?▼
Channel creators or Slack admins can rename channels. Submit a ticket via the form in #help-it.
